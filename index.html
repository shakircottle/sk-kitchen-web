The Corpse Reviver No. 2

$8

The Corpse Reviver No. 2 belongs to a family of pre-Prohibition cocktails that were consumed for the chief purpose of rousing the drinker from the dead. In other words: They were meant to cure hangovers, increase vigor and otherwise improve one’s morning. This pick-me-up features gin, Lillet blanc (a French aperitif), orange liqueur and fresh lemon juice.
