Our Story

Established in 2020

Welcome to SK Kitchen. Made up of Shakir Cottle and Kayla Di Venosa. Two strong and hardworking individuals just trying to cook up the best relationship they can. Actions and words filled with care and love, we try to better one another each and everyday. Using only the finest manners and etiquette to treat each other with kindness and respect.

These two love to have fun. Kayla being the more wild of the two, pushes this duo to try exciting new things. Her bright energy, passion for travel and spontaneity is what truly breathes life into this kitchen.

Our Goals

We strive to continue to better each other, grow positively together and work our health mentally and physically. To build a fortress of a relationship. Something so strong and impentrable that nothing can ever get between us. To shine on one another, even when one of us is down, to be bright enough to shine for the both of us. To build an ironclad relationship that will never break as it was forged in fire. With love. Always

Only the finest food and drinks, curated with the help from the one and only Chef Steven Faria. Specifically to tailor the tastebuds of one beautiful Kayla Di Venosa. With Love. Shakir <3
